# Phantom Files: Last Day

A Persona 5 inspired visual novel made with Ren'Py.

## Story

Aoyama Academy. Three months ago, your secret group — the Phantom Files — 
was exposed and your closest ally Ren was suspended pending expulsion. 
Someone betrayed you. Today is the last day before the hearing, and you 
have one chance to uncover the truth before everything falls apart forever.

## Characters

- **You** — the protagonist, a former member of the Phantom Files
- **Ren** — your suspended ally, bitter and exhausted
- **Akira** — the student council president, hiding something
- **Yuki** — the quiet art student who sees everything
- **Sensei Mori** — a teacher who knows more than he lets on

## Endings

There are 4 endings to discover:
-  Phantom Ending
-  Shattered Ending
-  Hollow Ending
-  Secret Ending (find it yourself...)
