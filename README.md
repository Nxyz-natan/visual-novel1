# Phantom Files: Last Day

A Persona 5 inspired visual novel made with Ren'Py.

## About

Phantom Files: Last Day is a mystery visual novel set in Aoyama Academy.
You play as a former member of a secret student group called the Phantom Files.
Three months ago, someone betrayed your group and your closest ally Ren was suspended.
Today is the last day before the expulsion hearing.
You have one day to find the truth.

## Story

Aoyama Academy feels different now. The hallways that used to feel like yours
now feel like enemy territory. Everyone stares Somewhere in this
building a traitor is living their normal life.

## Characters

- **You**  the mc, a former Phantom Files member searching for the truth
- **Ren**  your suspended friend, exhausted and bitter after three months of waiting
- **Akira** the student council president, defensive and hiding something
- **Yuki**  the observant quiet art student 
- **Sensei Mori**  a teacher (They might know more than they look like)

## Endings

There are 3 endings to discover depending on your choices:

- Phantom Ending
- Shattered Ending
- Hollow Ending

Can you find the truth before it is too late?
